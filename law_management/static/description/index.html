Law and Legal Practice Management

This module allows you to manage the process of Law management.

  • Client Requests
  • Lawyer Profile
  • Client Details
  • Judge Informations
  • Opposition Lawyers Details
  • Management of Matters
  • Management of Trials
  • Matter Invoicing

Client Request Process

  • Using this user can request for the services.
  • After approval of the application, it's converted as a client.
  • The Client can request from the listed Legal Services.
  • And can describe the Matter details, which once approved by admin automatic client record for it is generated for that client.

Approved client

  • The Client form helps to track the Invoicing related information.
  • Can directly track the Invoiced and Overdue amount, or can charge them by creating a new Invoice.

Lawyer Information

  • Lawyers can specify the Practice Area and fees based on an hour or trial.
  • The lawyer will have many other details such as their Experience, Qualification and Personal Information.

Matter Details

  • The matter is used to managing the Case Number and its trials.
  • In matter, you can manage its Victims, perpetrators, suspects, act, and evidence related to the matter.

Report for Analysis

  • PDF reports for Lawyer and Matter details which display information graphically for better analysis.
